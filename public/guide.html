CaseConnect

Find a case-interview partner and book a time — in about a minute.

Open CaseConnect →
Step 1

Sign in — no password needed

Enter your name and BYU email, then pick how many cases you've done. Your level shows as a chess piece so partners know what to expect.

♟ 0–5 ♞ 6–15 ♝ 16–30 ♜ 31–50 ♛ 51+ ♚ Didn't count
  • Coming back? Just start typing your name and tap your entry to auto-fill everything.
  • Your info is remembered on your device, so next time is one tap.
Step 2

Set up your session

On the Your Time tab, choose what you want to practice and your time zone.

  • Topic — Profitability, Market Sizing, M&A… plus Coffee Chat / Casual Chat, Whatever, or Custom (type your own).
  • Role — Interviewer, Interviewee, or Flexible.
  • Time zone — defaults to Mountain. Pick yours and every invite converts automatically (great for friends out of state or abroad).
Step 3

Paint your availability

Drag across the calendar to select your free 30-minute blocks — like painting. Switch between 3-Day, Work Week, Week, and Month, and book days or weeks ahead. Try it right here:

Step 4

Find a partner

Open Find Partner and choose how to look:

  • ⚡ Smart Match — instantly shows people who want the same topic and overlap with your free times.
  • 🔍 Discover — browse everyone and filter by case type.
  • Each person's card shows their level, topic, and open time slots. Tap a slot to book it.
Step 5

Get your calendar invite

The moment you book, a confirmation pops up. Add it to your calendar in one tap — your partner is invited automatically.

Quick questions

Is it free?

Completely. No accounts to pay for, no ads, nothing to install.

Do I need a password?

No — just your name and email to identify your schedule. Easy in, easy out.

Does it work on my phone?

Yes. It works in any browser on phone, tablet, or laptop.

Whose time zone are the times in?

Each person sets their own. When you book someone, the calendar invite is created in the correct time for both of you automatically.
